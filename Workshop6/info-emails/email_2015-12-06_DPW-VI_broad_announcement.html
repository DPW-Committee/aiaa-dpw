The 6th AIAA CFD Drag Prediction Workshop will take place Thursday June 16 and Friday June 17, 2016 during the AIAA Aviation 2016 conference in Washington DC. The AIAA Organizers will schedule Applied Aerodynamics sessions Monday through Wednesday to avoid a conflict.
