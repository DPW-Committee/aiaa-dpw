ONERA OAT15A Airfoil

The ONERA OAT15A airfoil is being used for Test Case 1: Solver Validation. Use of the ONERA OAT15A airfoil is subject to the following conditions

  • The geometry remains the intellectual property of ONERA
  • The geometry shall be referred to as the "ONERA OAT15A"
  • ONERA will be acknowledged each time the geometry is shared with a partner
  • When shared by a user, the above conditions will be included
The geometry, discrete coordinates, and an iges file, are available for download. Participants are strongly encouraged to use the provided iges file (ONERA-OAT15A_230mmChord_780mmSpan_upZ_2024_09_05.igs) as great care has been taken to develop a carefully-crafted geometry with good curvature behavior.
