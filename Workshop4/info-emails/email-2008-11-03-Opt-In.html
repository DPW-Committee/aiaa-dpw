Welcome to the 4th AIAA CFD Drag Prediction Workshop

Sponsored by the Applied Aerodynamics TC

Preceding the 27th APA Conference

June 20-21, 2009

San Antonio, TX

 

This is an Opt-In notification.  If you wish to be included in any further notifications, please reply to dpw@cessna.textron.com to let us know.  Please forward this message to anyone you know who may be interested.

 

This workshop will focus on the NASA Common Research Model (CRM) transonic wing-body-tail geometry.  More information can be found at our web site: http://aiaa-dpw.larc.nasa.gov/

 

The geometries are now available for download.  The organizing committee will be building grids to be made available for participants to use.  Anyone else interested in providing grids for general participant use, please let us know.

 

Regards,

DPW Organizing Committee
