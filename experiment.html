Experimental References

Experimental data sets are available and much has been published by many organizations.


Common Research Model (CRM)

Data for the Common Research Model from the National Transonic Faciilty (NTF) and European Transonic Wind Tunnel (ETW) can be found in a data set search engine. An overview of these tests was presented at AVIATION 2023.

JAXA data can be downloaded from the JAXA site. Geometry is available on the geometry page.


ONERA OAT15A Airfoil

The experimental data, airfoil coordinates, and IGES geometry as well as the supporting paper is Jaquin, et al. "Experimental Study of Shock Oscillation over a Transonic Supercritical Profiles." AIAA Journal, Vol. 47, No. 9, 2009. Pages 1985-1994.
