We would like to know if there is anyone else planning to submit data. Data received by start-of-business Monday, 15-May-2006, will be included in the statistical analysis. Data received after Monday will be included in the post-workshop analysis to be presented at the ASM conference in Reno.

For any data received before the workshop, we will try to provide time for a presentation. Presentations will not be allowed if data has not been received.

Presentations

As in the past workshops, the presentation time will be between 15 and 20 minutes. Common areas such as geometry will be discussed by the DPW Committee and should be excluded.


Registration
