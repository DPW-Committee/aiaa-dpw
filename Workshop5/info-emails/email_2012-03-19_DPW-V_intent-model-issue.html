In addition, it has recently come to our attention that the wing of the CRM wind-tunnel model was built to the theoretical 1-G twist.  As a consequence, there is about 1 degree of additional washout at the wingtip due to aerodynamic load at the design condition of M=0.85 and CL=0.5 at both Reynolds numbers of 5 and 20 million per Cref.  The effect of this delta twist has been studied with two different CFD methods, both confirm that the effect on the drag polar is minor.  Furthermore, we emphasize that this issue in no way affects the validity of the DPW-5 Test Cases, nor does it require any additional action on your part.  The baseline grids for DPW-5 are still the same as those made available in January 2012.  Hence, any calculations that you may have already performed are valid.  Finally, a presentation will be provided at the workshop which discloses the details of the effects of this twist delta on lift, drag and pitching moment.
